Vampire Facial

The vampire facial near you, also known as PRP (Platelet Rich Plasma) facial, is a cosmetic treatment designed to rejuvenate the skin. By drawing a small amount of the patient’s blood, separating the platelet-rich plasma using centrifuge, and then application of the plasma on the face after the micro-needling session. The PRP, growth factors, stimulates collagen production and cell turnover, leading to improved skin texture, tone, and reduced wrinkles and scars on the face
